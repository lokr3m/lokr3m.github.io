Popular Coffee Drinks

Espresso

A strong, full-flavored coffee served in "shots".

Ingredients:

  • Finely ground coffee
  • Hot water

Americano

A coffee drink similar to drip coffee but made by adding hot water to espresso.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Hot water

Cappuccino

A balanced coffee drink with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk and milk foam

Latte

A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk and milk foam

Mocha

A chocolate-flavored coffee drink made with espresso.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso and steamed milk
  • Chocolate syrup or cocoa powder

Macchiato

An espresso-based drink with a small amount of milk.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk or milk foam
